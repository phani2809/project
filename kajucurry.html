Kaju Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Soak cashew nuts in warm water for 1 hour. Then, grind them into a smooth paste.

Step 2: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.

Step 3: Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

Step 4: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell goes away.

Step 5: Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.

Step 6: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.

Step 7: Add cashew nut paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.

Step 8: Add yogurt and cream. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 9: Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 10: Serve hot with rice or roti.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 1 hour (including soaking time)

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
