Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk, or as needed to make the dough
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda. Mix well.

Step 2: Add ghee and mix until it resembles breadcrumbs.

Step 3: Slowly add milk and knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.

Step 4: Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by heating sugar, water, cardamom powder, and rose water in a pan. Let it simmer until it reaches a sticky consistency.

Step 5: Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls and roll them smooth.

Step 6: Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat. Fry the dough balls until golden brown.

Step 7: Drain and immediately immerse them into the warm sugar syrup.

Step 8: Let the Gulab Jamuns soak in the syrup for at least 1-2 hours before serving.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
