Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 cup strawberries, chopped
  • 1 cup grapes, halved
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup mango, diced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine all the diced and chopped fruits.

Step 2: Drizzle honey and lemon juice over the fruits.

Step 3: Gently toss the fruits until they are evenly coated with honey and lemon juice.

Step 4: Refrigerate the fruit salad for at least 30 minutes before serving to enhance the flavors.

Serving Suggestion

Serve the fruit salad chilled and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Note

You can customize this fruit salad recipe by adding or substituting fruits according to your preference.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Chilling Time: 30 minutes
