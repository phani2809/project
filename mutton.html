Mutton Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, washed and cleaned
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, chopped mint leaves, chopped cilantro, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2: Heat ghee in a pressure cooker. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.

Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.

Step 4: Add marinated mutton and cook for 5-6 minutes until the mutton is browned.

Step 5: Add soaked basmati rice and water. Mix well.

Step 6: Close the pressure cooker lid and cook for 2 whistles on medium heat.

Step 7: Once the pressure releases naturally, open the lid and gently fluff the biryani with a fork.

Step 8: Serve hot with raita and salad.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes (excluding marination time)

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
