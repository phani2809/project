Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 2 sprigs curry leaves
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine chicken pieces with ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, lemon juice, egg, cornflour, all-purpose flour, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.

Step 3: Deep fry the marinated chicken pieces until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step 4: In another pan, heat a little oil and add mustard seeds. When they start to crackle, add slit green chilies and curry leaves.

Step 5: Add the fried chicken pieces to the pan along with garam masala and optional yogurt. Toss everything together until well coated and heated through.

Step 6: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Marination Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
