Veg Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onions. Sauté until the onions become translucent.

Step 2: Add the chopped carrots, beans, bell peppers, and green peas. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

Step 3: Add the corn kernels to the pot and mix well. Pour in the vegetable broth or water and bring it to a boil.

Step 4: Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for about 10-15 minutes or until all the vegetables are cooked through.

Step 5: In a small bowl, mix the cornflour with 1/4 cup of water to make a smooth slurry. Slowly add this slurry to the soup, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

Step 6: Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Let the soup simmer for another 2-3 minutes until it thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 25 minutes
