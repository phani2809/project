Fish Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (any firm white fish like tilapia or cod)
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the fish with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, chopped mint leaves, chopped cilantro, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat ghee in a large pan or pressure cooker. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.

Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.

Step 4: Add marinated fish pieces and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until they are partially cooked.

Step 5: Layer soaked basmati rice over the partially cooked fish in the pan.

Step 6: Pour water over the rice until it is completely submerged.

Step 7: Cover the pan with a lid and cook on low heat for about 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the fish is fully cooked.

Step 8: Serve hot with raita and salad.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes (excluding marination time)

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
