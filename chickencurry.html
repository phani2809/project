Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

Step 2: Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saute until onions turn golden brown.

Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.

Step 4: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.

Step 5: Add chicken pieces and yogurt. Cook covered until chicken is tender.

Step 6: Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 7: Serve hot with rice or roti.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
