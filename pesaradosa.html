Pesara Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole green gram (moong dal)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 inch piece of ginger
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Recipe

Step 1: Rinse the whole green gram and soak it in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Drain the soaked green gram and transfer it to a blender. Add green chilies, ginger, and asafoetida. Blend to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.

Step 3: Transfer the batter to a bowl. Add salt and mix well.

Step 4: Heat a non-stick pan or a cast-iron skillet over medium heat.

Step 5: Pour a ladleful of batter onto the center of the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.

Step 6: Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges of the dosa. Cook until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown.

Step 7: Flip the dosa and cook for another minute or until done. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 10 minutes (excluding soaking time)

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
