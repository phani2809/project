Veg Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large pan, heat a little oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.

Step 2: Add the grated cabbage, grated carrots, and bell peppers to the pan. Stir well and cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

Step 3: Add the boiled and mashed potatoes to the vegetable mixture. Stir in the soy sauce and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Cook the mixture for another 3-4 minutes, ensuring everything is well combined. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

Step 5: Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface. Put a small amount of the vegetable mixture in the center of the wrapper. Fold in the sides and roll it up tightly. Seal the edges with a little water or a paste made of flour and water.

Step 6: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the rolls until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
