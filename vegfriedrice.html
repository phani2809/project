Veg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover rice)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, corn)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or any cooking oil)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper powder to taste
  • 2 spring onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Add minced garlic and chopped green chilies (if using). Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

Step 3: Add finely chopped onion and sauté until translucent.

Step 4: Add mixed vegetables to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are slightly tender.

Step 5: Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add cooked rice to the empty space. Break up any clumps and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

Step 6: Combine the vegetables and rice in the pan. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and black pepper powder. Mix well until everything is evenly coated.

Step 7: Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 8: Garnish with chopped spring onions and cilantro.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
