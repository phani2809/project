Egg Masala Curry

Ingredients

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.

Step 2: Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

Step 3: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell goes away.

Step 4: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.

Step 5: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.

Step 6: Add yogurt and coconut milk. Cook for 2-3 minutes.

Step 7: Add boiled eggs and cook covered for another 5 minutes.

Step 8: Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 9: Serve hot with rice or roti.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
