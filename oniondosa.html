Onion Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Recipe

Step 1: Rinse the rice, urad dal, poha, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak them in water for at least 4-6 hours.

Step 2: Drain the soaked ingredients and grind them together to form a smooth batter. Add water as needed to achieve a pouring consistency.

Step 3: Add salt to the batter and mix well. Cover and let it ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.

Step 4: Once fermented, stir the batter gently. Heat a non-stick pan or a cast-iron skillet over medium heat.

Step 5: Pour a ladleful of batter onto the center of the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.

Step 6: Sprinkle the chopped onions, green chilies, and coriander leaves evenly over the dosa.

Step 7: Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges of the dosa. Cook until the edges start to lift and the bottom turns golden brown.

Step 8: Flip the dosa and cook for another minute or until the onions are lightly browned. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes (excluding soaking and fermentation time)

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
