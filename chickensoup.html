Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onions are translucent.

Step 2: Add chopped carrots and celery to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes until they begin to soften.

Step 3: Add the chicken breasts to the pot, followed by the chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 4: Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred them using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

Step 5: Add corn kernels, peas, dried thyme, dried parsley, and the bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the soup simmer for another 10-15 minutes.

Step 6: Remove the bay leaf from the pot. Adjust seasoning if necessary.

Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes
