Butterscotch Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup butterscotch chips
  • Pinch of salt

Recipe

Step 1: In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the brown sugar and stir until dissolved.

Step 2: Pour in the heavy cream and whole milk. Stir until well combined.

Step 3: Add the butterscotch chips and continue stirring until they are melted and the mixture is smooth.

Step 4: Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.

Step 5: Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until well chilled.

Step 6: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions, usually about 20-25 minutes.

Step 7: Transfer the churned ice cream into an airtight container and freeze for at least 2 hours or until firm.

Step 8: Scoop and serve the butterscotch ice cream in bowls or cones, and enjoy!

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Chilling Time: 2 hours

Churning Time: 25 minutes

Freezing Time: 2 hours
