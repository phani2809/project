Black Forest Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup pitted cherries, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Recipe

Step 1: In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and unsweetened cocoa powder. Whisk until the mixture is smooth.

Step 2: Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring frequently, until the mixture comes to a simmer. Do not let it boil.

Step 3: Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the chopped cherries, chocolate chips, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Stir until the chocolate is completely melted and the mixture is smooth.

Step 4: Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until well chilled.

Step 5: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions, usually about 20-25 minutes.

Step 6: Transfer the churned ice cream into an airtight container and freeze for at least 2 hours or until firm.

Step 7: Scoop and serve the black forest ice cream in bowls or cones, and enjoy!

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Chilling Time: 2 hours

Churning Time: 25 minutes

Freezing Time: 2 hours
