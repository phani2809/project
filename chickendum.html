Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • Spices: cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaves
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a large pot and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

Step 3: In the same pot, add whole spices, ginger garlic paste, and saut√© for a few minutes.

Step 4: Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.

Step 5: Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, turmeric powder, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until the chicken is done.

Step 6: In a separate pot, boil the soaked rice with some salt until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.

Step 7: Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken curry. Sprinkle fried onions, chopped coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk over the rice.

Step 8: Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.

Step 9: Serve hot with raita or salad.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour
