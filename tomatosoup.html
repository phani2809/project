Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and saut√© until they become soft and translucent.

Step 2: Add chopped carrots and cook for another 5 minutes until they begin to soften.

Step 3: Add the chopped tomatoes to the pot. Stir well and cook for about 10 minutes until the tomatoes start breaking down.

Step 4: Pour in the vegetable broth, add sugar, and season with salt and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.

Step 5: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender and puree until smooth. Return the soup to the pot.

Step 6: Stir in the heavy cream if using, and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Heat the soup for a few more minutes, but do not let it boil.

Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
