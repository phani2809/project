Chicken Lollipops

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken wings, frenched
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, ginger garlic paste, salt, and black pepper powder.

Step 2: Add the chicken lollipops to the marinade and coat well. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 3: In a separate bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, and beaten egg to form a batter.

Step 4: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.

Step 5: Dip each marinated chicken lollipop into the batter, ensuring it is well coated.

Step 6: Deep fry the chicken lollipops in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.

Step 7: Remove the chicken lollipops from the oil and drain on paper towels.

Step 8: Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Marination Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
