Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water for syrup
  • 1/4 tsp saffron strands
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • Sliced pistachios or almonds for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, gram flour, yogurt, and water to make a smooth batter. Cover and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.

Step 2: After fermentation, add baking powder to the batter and mix well.

Step 3: In a separate saucepan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, saffron strands, and cardamom powder until it reaches one string consistency. Keep warm.

Step 4: Heat oil or ghee in a frying pan over medium heat.

Step 5: Pour the batter into a piping bag or a squeeze bottle with a narrow opening.

Step 6: Squeeze the batter in a circular motion into the hot oil to form spiral shapes.

Step 7: Fry until the jalebis are crisp and golden brown on both sides.

Step 8: Drain the fried jalebis and immediately immerse them into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for a minute.

Step 9: Remove the jalebis from the syrup and garnish with sliced pistachios or almonds.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 10 minutes (excluding fermentation time)

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
