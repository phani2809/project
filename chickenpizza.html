Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 200g cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (red, green, yellow)
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 220°C (428°F).

Step 2: Place the pizza base on a baking tray or pizza stone.

Step 3: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base.

Step 4: Sprinkle a generous amount of grated mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.

Step 5: Arrange the shredded chicken, bell peppers, onions, black olives, sweet corn, and mushrooms over the cheese.

Step 6: Sprinkle dried oregano and dried basil over the toppings. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 7: Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown.

Step 8: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes
