Paneer Butter Masala Curry

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

Step 2: Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saute until onions turn golden brown.

Step 3: Add chopped tomatoes and cashew nuts. Cook until tomatoes are soft.

Step 4: Let the mixture cool down, then blend it into a smooth paste.

Step 5: Transfer the paste back to the pan. Add garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.

Step 6: Add cubed paneer and heavy cream. Mix well and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.

Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
