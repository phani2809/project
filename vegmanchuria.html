Veg Manchuria

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped beans
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped capsicum
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped spring onions
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine grated cabbage, carrots, chopped beans, and capsicum. Add cornflour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, pepper powder, and salt. Mix well to form a dough-like consistency.

Step 2: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Shape the vegetable mixture into small balls and deep fry them until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and set aside.

Step 3: In another pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil. Add finely chopped spring onions and saut√© for a minute.

Step 4: Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, and a little water. Mix well and let it simmer for a few minutes.

Step 5: Add the fried vegetable balls to the sauce and toss well to coat them evenly.

Step 6: Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot as a snack or with fried rice or noodles.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
