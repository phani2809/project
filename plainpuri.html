Puri

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil, for deep frying

Recipe

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt. Add the oil or ghee and mix well.

Step 2: Gradually add water and knead the mixture into a firm, smooth dough. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.

Step 3: Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls.

Step 4: Roll each ball into a small circle about 4-5 inches in diameter. Ensure the puris are not too thin.

Step 5: Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat. To test if the oil is hot enough, drop a small piece of dough into the oil; it should rise to the surface immediately.

Step 6: Gently slide one puri into the hot oil. It should puff up immediately. Use a slotted spoon to gently press down on the puri to help it puff up fully.

Step 7: Fry the puri until it turns golden brown on both sides. Remove it with a slotted spoon and place it on paper towels to drain excess oil.

Step 8: Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls. Serve hot with your favorite curry or side dish.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
