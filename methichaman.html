Methi Chaman Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh fenugreek leaves (methi)
  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Wash and chop the fenugreek leaves and spinach.

Step 2: Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

Step 3: Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Saute until onions turn golden brown.

Step 4: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft.

Step 5: Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.

Step 6: Add chopped fenugreek leaves and spinach. Cook until they wilt.

Step 7: Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Mix gently.

Step 8: Finally, add fresh cream, mix well, and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

Step 9: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
