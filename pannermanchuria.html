Paneer Manchuria

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp pepper powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: In a bowl, mix cornflour, all-purpose flour, (optional) egg, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, pepper powder, and salt. Add paneer cubes and mix well to coat. Marinate for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Deep fry the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and set aside.

Step 3: In another pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil. Add chopped onions, capsicum, and green chilies. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, and vinegar. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.

Step 5: Add the fried paneer pieces to the sauce and toss well to coat them evenly.

Step 6: Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot as a starter or with fried rice or noodles.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
