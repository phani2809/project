Veg Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 veggie patties
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Slice of tomato
  • Slice of onion
  • Slice of cheese
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard sauce
  • Butter for toasting
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Toast the burger buns lightly on a skillet with butter.

Step 2: In a separate pan, cook the veggie patties according to package instructions.

Step 3: Mix mayonnaise, tomato ketchup, and mustard sauce in a bowl to make the sauce.

Step 4: Spread the sauce on the bottom half of each toasted bun.

Step 5: Layer lettuce leaves, tomato slices, onion slices, and cheese slice on top of the sauce.

Step 6: Place the cooked veggie patty on top of the cheese.

Step 7: Cover with the top half of the bun.

Step 8: Serve hot with your favorite sides.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes
