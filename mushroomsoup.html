Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: In a large pot, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onions are translucent.

Step 2: Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook until they are browned and their moisture has evaporated, about 10 minutes.

Step 3: Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir well to combine. Cook for another 2 minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.

Step 4: Gradually add the vegetable broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Step 5: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender and puree until smooth. Return the soup to the pot.

Step 6: Stir in the heavy cream, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Heat the soup for a few more minutes, but do not let it boil.

Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Time Required

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes
