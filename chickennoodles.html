Chicken Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from pan and set aside.

Step 3: In the same pan, add more oil if needed. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until fragrant.

Step 4: Add carrot, bell pepper, and cabbage. Cook for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.

Step 5: Add cooked noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce to the pan. Stir well to combine.

Step 6: Return cooked chicken to the pan. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.

Step 7: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Time Required

Cooking Time: 20 minutes
